About Happy Tails Hub

More than a decade of providing exceptional, compassionate care for pets and peace of mind for their families

Our Story

Happy Tails Hub was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality veterinary care in a warm, welcoming environment where pets and their families feel truly cared for.

What started as a small neighborhood clinic has grown into a full-service pet care facility, but our core values remain the same. We believe that pets are family members who deserve compassionate, comprehensive care from professionals who understand the special bond between pets and their people.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of pets and their families, offering everything from routine wellness care to advanced medical treatments.
